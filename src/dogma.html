About

Etch photo was created for WEB 2620. I have had an interest in photography. The goal of the website is to explore photography in a focused hands-on manner. A requirement of the project was that the website be responsive to three screen sizes.

The Etch website is the largest project I have worked on. The time expended to find resources and consider the design are also notable.

The images I used were pulled from bluemooncamera.com and magnumphoto.com
